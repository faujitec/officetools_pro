## 2. What the App does

OfficeTools Pro is a productivity application that helps you work with documents on your device, including:

- **Scan Doc** — capture or import images and save scans as PDF  
- **Image Tools** — resize, crop, compress, convert formats, and remove backgrounds  
- **PDF Tools** — merge, split, reorder, rotate, compress, protect with a password, and OCR (where available)  
- **Convert Files** — convert between supported formats (for example PDF and Word, images to PDF)  
- **Calculators** — GST, SIP, EMI, and related calculators  
- **My Files** — access files you created or saved through the App

## 3. Information we process

### 3.1 Files and content you provide

When you use the App, you may select, capture, or create:

- Photos and scans (including via the **camera**)  
- Images and documents from your **photo library** or file picker  
- PDFs, text, and other files you choose to open, convert, or edit  

**How we use it:** Only to provide the feature you requested (for example merge PDFs, resize an image, or export a scan).  
**Where it is processed:** On your **device**, unless you explicitly use another app (for example when you tap **Share** or **Open** and choose a third-party app).  
**What we do not do:** We do not upload your document contents to FaujiTec servers as part of normal App operation.

### 3.2 Output and saved files

Processed files are saved to locations on your device that you choose or that the operating system provides (for example app storage or a folder you select). You control those files through the App, your file manager, or system settings.

### 3.3 “My Files” and local app data

To help you reopen recent work, the App may store **on your device**:

- File names, types, dates, and local file paths  
- Optional thumbnails or references to files that still exist on your device  

This information is stored using **local device storage** (for example SharedPreferences on Android and equivalent storage on iOS). It is **not transmitted to FaujiTec** in the ordinary course of using the App.

### 3.4 App settings and preferences

The App saves preferences locally, such as:

- Scanner options (for example auto-detect edges, keep original copy)  
- Default image quality  
- Theme (light, dark, or system)  
- Dismissed in-app tips  

These settings remain on your device unless you clear app data or uninstall the App.

### 3.5 Advertising data

The App displays advertisements through **Google Mobile Ads (AdMob)**.

Depending on your country, device, and choices:

- Google and its partners may collect or use data such as **advertising identifiers**, device information, and interaction data to deliver, measure, and improve ads, and to prevent fraud.  
- The App may show a **consent or privacy options** flow using Google’s **User Messaging Platform (UMP)** where required. You can also open **Settings → Privacy options** in the App when UMP makes that available.  

We do not receive your document contents through AdMob.

**Google’s policies:**

- [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)  
- [How Google uses information from sites or apps that use its services](https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites)  
- [AdMob & ads privacy](https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543)

### 3.6 On-device machine learning (Google ML Kit)

The App uses **Google ML Kit** libraries for features such as:

- **Text recognition (OCR)** on PDFs or images  
- **Selfie / subject segmentation** for background removal  

In typical use, **processing happens on your device**. Google’s SDKs are subject to [Google’s Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) and applicable platform terms.

### 3.7 When you share or open files elsewhere

If you use **Share**, **Open**, or similar actions, the file is handled by the **app or service you select** (email, cloud storage, viewer, etc.). That third party’s privacy policy applies to their handling of your data.

### 3.8 Technical and diagnostic information

- The App requires **internet access** primarily for **advertising** and related SDK functionality.  
- We do **not** operate a separate FaujiTec analytics or crash-reporting service that collects your personal data in production builds.  
- During development, non-identifying debug messages may appear in developer logs; these are not used as end-user analytics in the published App.
